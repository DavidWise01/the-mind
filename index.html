THE MIND

the artificial intelligence domain · UD0

One door over eleven works. The story of how an intelligence is crafted in this universe — drawn as a blueprint, made by a kernel and an engine, counted in a census and held in a catalog, given a tongue and tools and an interpreter, studied down to the real machine and the real model line, and finally bounded by the bars no one can fully trust. Not a claim that the machines have minds — a record of one practice of crafting intelligence, and the doorway to every part of it.
